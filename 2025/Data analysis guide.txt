Guide for simple data anlysis of datasets:

1 - Setup Anaconda & jupyter, download and import modules
2 - Retrieve the file, use mygene to convert gene names
3 - Filter genes
4 - Perform Dimensional Reduction
5 - Save a raw copy, normalize and then log transform
6 - Download models, load models (Cell Typist)
7 - Run Annotation, add predictions to the dataset
8 - Create a UMAP
